CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file should include the following columns:

  • first_name (required)
  • last_name (required)
  • email (optional)
  • company (optional)
  • phone (optional)
  • job_title (optional)

Additional columns will be ignored. Duplicate emails will be skipped.

Sample CSV Format:
