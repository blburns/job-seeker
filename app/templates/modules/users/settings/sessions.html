Session Management
Session Duration

Standard sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity. With "Remember Me" enabled, sessions last for 30 days.

Security

Each session is tracked with device information and IP address. Suspicious activity will trigger security alerts.

Multiple Devices

You can be signed in on multiple devices simultaneously. Each device maintains its own session.

Sign Out

Signing out revokes the session immediately. You'll need to sign in again to access your account.
